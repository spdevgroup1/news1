HEADLINE
Erika Eleniak looks totally different

SUMMARY
“Baywatch” alum Erika Eleniak looks totally different in her edgy new film role decades after playing a bombshell lifeguard on the hit show.
Eleniak, 56 — who played Shauni McClain in the first three seasons of the ’90s surf series — flaunted her sleeves of impressive ink via Instagram in a photo from her upcoming film Alchemy In Venice.
